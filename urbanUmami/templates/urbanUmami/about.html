ABOUT US

At Urban Umami, we bring together the rich flavors of Indian, Chinese, and Continental cuisines under one vibrant roof. Our goal is to create a dining experience that celebrates global taste while delivering comfort, quality, and creativity in every bite.

Indian Cuisine: We offer a wide range of authentic Indian dishes, from aromatic curries and flavorful biryanis to sizzling tandoor specials. Every recipe reflects the true essence of Indian spices, tradition, and warmth.

Chinese Cuisine: Our Chinese menu features classic favorites like noodles, fried rice, dim sum, and expertly crafted stir-fries. With perfect balance, fresh ingredients, and bold flavors, we bring you the best of Asian-inspired cooking.

Continental Cuisine: For those who enjoy subtle, elegant flavors, our Continental dishes showcase creamy sauces, grilled specialties, soups, salads, and perfectly plated meals. It’s a modern take on Western-style cuisine with a touch of sophistication.

At Urban Umami, we believe great food connects people. That’s why our chefs combine passion, skill, and creativity to offer a menu that satisfies every taste. Whether you’re craving something traditional, international, or comforting, Urban Umami is your destination for delicious moments.
